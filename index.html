About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan Hitt. I'm a web designer and front-end developer based in Little Rock, Arkansas.

After spending more than fifteen years in leadership and customer service roles, I decided to return to school to pursue a career in web design and development. While I originally attended UA Little Rock as a graphic design major in 2008, my music career took me in a different direction for many years before I returned to complete my degree.

Through my coursework and freelance projects, I've developed experience building responsive websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress, Elementor, and GitHub. I enjoy creating clean, user-focused websites that balance thoughtful design with practical functionality.

Today, I'm focused on helping individuals, small businesses, and organizations build modern websites that are responsive, accessible, and easy to maintain while continuing to grow my skills as a front-end developer.
