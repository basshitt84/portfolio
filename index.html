About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan Hitt. I am a senior majoring in Web Design and Developement.

When I first started this program, I was definitely a little nervous. It had been years since I’d been in school, and jumping into something new felt like a big move. I originally came to UA Little Rock back in 2008 as a graphic design major, but my music career took off and I followed that path for a long time. I’m still an active professional musician, but eventually I wanted something more stable and future-focused. Coming back to school made sense.

Choosing web design and development was the part that happened a bit on a whim — I knew the job market was strong, and I’d always been curious about how websites were built. Once I got into the coursework, I realized how much I enjoyed the problem-solving and creativity behind it. I still have a lot to learn, but each semester I’m gaining more experience and feeling more confident in my abilities.

Now I’m on track to graduate in Spring 2026, and I’m excited to see where this degree takes me next.
